Tips for Best Photos:

  • If taking headshots, consider hiring a professional make up artist.
  • Stick to clothes with solid colors. Patterns are dsicouraged as they may be distracting. Do not wear clothes with logos.
  • Prepare 2-3 outfits.
  • Avoid wearing sunscreen or make up with sunscreen. This will make your face paler than it appears, not to mention a shiny face is hard to edit.
  • Talk to your photographer about what you expect your photos results.
